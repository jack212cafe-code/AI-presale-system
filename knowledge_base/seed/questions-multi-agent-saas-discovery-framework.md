# Questions Multi-Agent SaaS Discovery Framework

- Is the primary buyer an SI, a distributor, or a mixed partner ecosystem, and how do their operating models differ?
- What business outcome matters most: faster presale cycle, standardized proposal quality, partner enablement, knowledge reuse, or recurring revenue from SaaS packaging?
- How should the SaaS offer be packaged: entry, growth, enterprise, or other tiering based on users, features, agents, integrations, or support level?
- Does the customer expect monthly, yearly, or mixed subscription models, and what renewal, upgrade, or downgrade path must be supported?
- What data isolation, tenant separation, audit, and access control expectations are required for partner-facing cloud usage?
- Which integrations are essential at launch: CRM, ticketing, identity provider, document storage, messaging, or workflow automation?
- What support, onboarding, and knowledge maintenance model is required so the SaaS offer remains commercially viable after launch?
