# Questions Security and Recovery Discovery Framework

- What security events or recovery failures have already happened, and what changed in priorities because of them?
- Which identities, systems, and data sets must be protected first during a cyber incident or DR event?
- What are the requirements for immutability, segmentation, MFA, logging retention, and privileged access review?
- How is recovery declared, who owns the decision, and what technical dependencies must survive for recovery to work?
- What controls are mandatory by policy or compliance, and what controls are optional improvements?
- How often are backup restores, DR tests, and operational failover exercises performed today?
- Where is the boundary between infrastructure resilience, cyber recovery, and security operations in the customer organization?
