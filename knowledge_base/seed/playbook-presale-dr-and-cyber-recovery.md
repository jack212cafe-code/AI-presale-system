# Playbook Presale DR and Cyber Recovery

- Separate classic DR from cyber recovery. A warm site for infrastructure continuity does not automatically satisfy ransomware recovery requirements.
- Confirm which applications must recover first, which data sets require immutability, how identity services survive, and what dependencies exist on DNS, certificates, or management tooling.
- Map recovery planning to real operations: replication health, isolation procedures, credential trust, clean-room expectations, restore testing cadence, and executive decision points.
- A credible DR or cyber recovery proposal includes not only target architecture, but also testing model, declaration process, and role ownership during an actual event.
- If the customer cannot define RTO, RPO, and service tiering, position the proposal as conditional and avoid pretending the architecture is fully sized.
