# Playbook Presale Discovery and Qualification

- Start by identifying business drivers, current pain, expected outcomes, and timeline pressure before discussing products. A technically valid solution can still fail if it does not map to the buying reason.
- Capture the current-state architecture: compute, storage, network, backup, security, cloud dependencies, operations team structure, and major incident history.
- Separate confirmed facts from assumptions. Use explicit wording for unknowns such as recovery target, data growth, application criticality, compliance needs, or platform ownership.
- Ask for sizing evidence, not rough feelings. Collect VM counts, storage growth, IOPS indicators, backup windows, bandwidth limits, site topology, and deployment constraints.
- End discovery with a gap list, a risk list, and the minimum technical data needed before final BOM or proposal commitment.
