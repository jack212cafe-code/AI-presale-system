# Vendor Cisco Switching and Fabric Guide

- Position Cisco switching when the customer prioritizes established operational models, broad feature depth, enterprise support expectations, and integration with mature routing and security practices.
- Validate the design at both underlay and overlay levels. Switching proposals must explain uplink resiliency, control-plane behavior, policy boundaries, and operational telemetry before claiming readiness for production.
- For fabric or SDN-driven proposals, identify the minimum operational maturity required from the customer team. Automation and policy centralization do not remove the need for change discipline and rollback planning.
- Include management-plane protection, AAA integration, configuration backup, software lifecycle, and interface-level observability in the baseline recommendation.
- For presale comparison, distinguish clearly between campus switching, data center fabric, and multi-site policy requirements rather than using one generic switching pitch.
