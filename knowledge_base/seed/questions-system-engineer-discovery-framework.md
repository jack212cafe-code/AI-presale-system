# Questions System Engineer Discovery Framework

- What business services are most critical, and what outage duration is actually unacceptable for them?
- What are the current server, storage, and virtualization platforms, and what pain points are driving the refresh?
- How many workloads are in scope, what are their growth trends, and which ones are latency or performance sensitive?
- What are the current backup, restore, and DR practices, and where does confidence break down today?
- What maintenance windows, rack space, power, cooling, and network constraints must the design respect?
- Which dependencies must remain available during failure or maintenance: identity, DNS, monitoring, management plane, backup targets, or replication services?
- What operational team will own the platform after go-live, and what level of complexity can they sustain safely?
