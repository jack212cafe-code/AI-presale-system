# Questions Network Engineer Discovery Framework

- What traffic patterns matter most: user access, east-west application flows, backup traffic, storage traffic, branch connectivity, or Internet breakout?
- How are segmentation and security boundaries handled today, and where are the biggest operational or compliance gaps?
- What switching and routing platforms are in place now, and what failures or limitations are forcing change?
- Does the customer need SDN or overlay networking for real operational reasons, or would a simpler routed design solve the business problem?
- What are the resiliency expectations for switches, uplinks, WAN paths, and controller or management components?
- Who owns network policy, firewall policy, and routing decisions across data center, branch, and cloud-connected environments?
- What telemetry, logging, and troubleshooting visibility is available today, and where do incidents become hard to isolate?
